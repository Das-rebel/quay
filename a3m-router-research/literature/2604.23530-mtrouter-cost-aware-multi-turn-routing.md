# MTRouter: Cost-Aware Multi-Turn LLM Routing with History-Model Joint Embeddings

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2604.23530
- **Authors**: Yiqun Zhang, Hao Li, Zihan Wang, Shi Feng, Xiaocui Yang, Daling Wang, Bo Zhang, Lei Bai, Shuyue Hu
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.CL, cs.AI
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23530

## Key Innovation
Multi-turn LLM routing using history-model joint embeddings. Captures both conversation history and model capabilities in a unified embedding space to make better routing decisions across turns. Explicitly cost-aware to minimize cumulative cost while maintaining quality.
