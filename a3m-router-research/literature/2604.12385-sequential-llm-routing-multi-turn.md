# From Myopic Selection to Long-Horizon Awareness: Sequential LLM Routing for Multi-Turn Dialogue

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2604.12385
- **Authors**: Jiarui Zhang, Xiangyu Liu, Yong Hu, Chaoyue Niu, Hang Zeng, Shaojie Tang, Fan Wu, Guihai Chen
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.CL
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12385

## Key Innovation
Addresses the limitation that most LLM routers make single-shot decisions ignoring the multi-turn nature of conversations. Proposes sequential routing that considers long-horizon dialogue context and future rewards rather than just immediate task completion.

## Relevance to A3M Router Improvement
- **High relevance** - Multi-turn routing is critical for conversational applications
- Could enhance A3M Router's ability to handle conversational flows
- May reduce total cost by optimizing across turns rather than per-turn

## Notes
Very relevant for WhatsApp/Telegram bot scenarios where multi-turn conversations are common.
