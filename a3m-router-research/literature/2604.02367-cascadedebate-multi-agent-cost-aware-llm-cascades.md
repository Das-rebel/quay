# CascadeDebate: Multi-Agent Deliberation for Cost-Aware LLM Cascades

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2604.02367
- **Authors**: Raeyoung Chang, Dongwook Kwon, Jisoo Lee, Nikhil Verma
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.NI, cs.CL
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02367

## Key Innovation
Multi-agent deliberation for cost-aware LLM cascades. Uses multiple models in a debate-style format to balance quality and cost. Coordinates models of varying sizes with human experts.

## Relevance to A3M Router Improvement
- **Medium relevance** - Cascading approach could complement routing
- Multi-agent deliberation is an interesting research direction
