# The Routing Plateau: Understanding and Breaking the Accuracy Limits of LLM Routers

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2606.07587
- **Authors**: Yifan Lu, Qiyue Zhang, Shenrun Zhang, Zhibo Yu, Zhuang Wang, Hanjie Chen, Jiarong Xing
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.LG
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07587

## Key Innovation
Analyzes fundamental accuracy limits of LLM routers - the "routing plateau" phenomenon. Investigates why routers struggle to exceed certain accuracy thresholds and proposes methods to break through these limits.

## Relevance to A3M Router Improvement
- **High relevance** - Understanding router limitations is key to improving them
- May reveal fundamental constraints that A3M Router faces
- Could guide architectural improvements
