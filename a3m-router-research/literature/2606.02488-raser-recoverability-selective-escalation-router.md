# RASER: Recoverability-Aware Selective Escalation Router for Multi-Hop Question Answering

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2606.02488
- **Authors**: Yuyang Li, Zihe Yan, Tobias Käfer
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.AI
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02488

## Key Innovation
Recoverability-aware selective escalation router for multi-hop QA. Routes queries based on whether they can be recovered from errors. Escalates to more capable models only when recovery is possible.

## Relevance to A3M Router Improvement
- **Medium relevance** - Interesting approach for selective escalation
- Could inspire cost-saving strategies by routing unsolvable queries
