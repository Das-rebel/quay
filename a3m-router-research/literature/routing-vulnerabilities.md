# Life-Cycle Routing Vulnerabilities of LLM Router

## Paper Details
- **arXiv**: 2503.08704
- **Key Finding**: Training-free routers are MORE robust than DNN-based routers

## Key Insight
- DNN-based routers have weakest adversarial robustness
- Training-free routers (like A3M's heuristic approach) are most robust
- But A3M could improve with better pattern matching for MCQ detection
