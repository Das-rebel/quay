# ReCal: Reward Calibration for RL-based LLM Routing

## Paper Details
- **arXiv ID**: 2606.12479
- **Authors**: Qihang Yu, Hanwen Tong, Zhengqi Zhang, Bo Zheng, Feng Wei, Shengyu Zhang, Zemin Liu, Fei Wu
- **Year**: 2026
- **Categories**: cs.LG, cs.AI
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12479

## Key Innovation
RL-based LLM routing with reward calibration. Addresses the problem that RL-trained routers suffer from reward model noise and distribution shift, leading to suboptimal routing decisions. Proposes a calibration method to correct reward signals before training the router.

## Relevance to A3M Router Improvement
- **High relevance** - Directly addresses RL-based routing which is the core of A3M Router
- Could improve the reward model used for training the router policy
- May provide better training signals for the router
